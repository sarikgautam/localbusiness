About FindSAB

Bridging language barriers and connecting communities worldwide

Our Mission

At FindSAB, our mission is to bridge the language and cultural gaps that newcomers and expatriates face when settling in a foreign country. We understand that finding professionals and businesses who speak your language can make all the difference in feeling at home in a new place.

We're dedicated to creating a platform that connects people with service providers who not only meet their needs but also understand their cultural background and speak their language. This shared understanding fosters trust, improves communication, and creates a sense of community for those living abroad.

Connecting through shared language

Our Story

From Personal Experience to Global Solution

FindSAB was born from the personal experiences of our founders who, as immigrants themselves, faced the challenges of finding service providers who could understand not just their needs, but also their language and cultural context.

After struggling to find a dentist who spoke their native language during a dental emergency, our founder realized there was a significant gap in connecting newcomers with language-compatible service providers. This experience sparked the idea for FindSAB – a platform specifically designed to help people find businesses and professionals who speak their language.

Today, FindSAB has grown from a simple idea into a comprehensive directory serving communities worldwide, helping thousands of people feel more at home wherever they are.
